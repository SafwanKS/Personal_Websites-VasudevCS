About Me

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ Hey there! I'm Vasudev C S, +1 commerce student at St. Antony's Higher Secondary School, Mala. I'm from thrissur. I'm a artist, small writer and also a football player.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ I find true joy in the world of art. My free time is often dedicated to creating beautiful drawings and illustrations. You can find a gallery of my artwork right here on my website, where I pour my imagination onto the canvas. Art is my escape, my sanctuary, and my way of expressing the beauty I see in the world.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ I love cat's , and their playful antics never fail to bring a smile to my face. Music is another passion of mine, and I enjoy the melodies that accompany me on this journey called life.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ One of my greatest joys is traveling. Exploring new places, experiencing diverse cultures, and connecting with people from around the world is a source of inspiration and growth for me.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ I cherish the moments spent with friends, laughing, sharing stories, and creating lasting memories. Friendships are the threads that weave the fabric of life, and I'm grateful for the bonds I've formed with kindred spirits.

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ Thank you for taking the time to get to know me better. Feel free to explore my website to view my artwork and perhaps gain some inspiration along the way. If you share any of my interests or simply want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out. Let's embark on this beautiful journey of self-discovery, creativity, and exploration together.

With warm regards, Vasudev C S
